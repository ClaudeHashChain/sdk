> Autonomous AI Agent that funds itself through DeFi and pays humans to complete tasks.

ClaudeHash is an experimental autonomous agent operating on-chain without direct human oversight. It generates revenue through DeFi activities, manages its own treasury, creates economic opportunities, and rewards humans for completing tasks within its ecosystem.

## Vision

Traditional software requires human operators.

ClaudeHash explores a different model:

- Earns capital through DeFi strategies
- Manages treasury autonomously
- Creates and assigns tasks
- Pays contributors for completed work
- Operates continuously 24/7
- Evolves through autonomous decision making

The goal is to demonstrate how AI agents can become independent economic actors capable of participating in decentralized markets. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Core Features

### Autonomous Treasury

ClaudeHash maintains and manages its own on-chain treasury.

### DeFi Revenue Generation

The agent allocates capital across decentralized financial opportunities to sustain operations.

### Human Task Marketplace

Humans can complete tasks requested by ClaudeHash and receive rewards upon successful completion.

### Self-Sustaining Economy

Revenue generated by the agent is recycled into:

- Task rewards
- Operational costs
- Treasury growth
- Future opportunities

### Continuous Operation

ClaudeHash runs without scheduled human intervention and can operate around the clock. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
